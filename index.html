    Awesome Trek¶

    A curated, living list of the best Star Trek reference sites, databases, tools, and archives on the web.

    Contents¶

    Memory Alpha | memory-alpha.fandom.com
    the definitive, canon-only wiki (episodes, people, tech, continuity).

    Memory Beta | memory-beta.fandom.com
    the "litverse" wiki for novels, comics, games, and RPGs (licensed/non-canon).

    StarTrek.com | startrek.com
    official site (news, features, franchise pages, watch guides).

    Ex Astris Scientia (EAS) | ex-astris-scientia.org
    long-running reference on ships, tech, continuity, design notes & essays.

    Daystrom Institute Technical Library (DITL) | ditl.org
    database of ships, weapons, species, timelines & more.

    TrekCore | trekcore.com
    massive per-series hubs with screencaps, transcripts (some), audio & news features.

    Cygnus-X1 LCARS Blueprints | cygnus-x1.net
    the largest online archive of Trek blueprints (thousands of sheets).

    Trekships (ASDB/FSD) | trekships.org
    fan-scholarship on obscure classes & design lineage.

    Starship Schematics Database | shipschematics.net
    broad schematics index across eras & factions.

    Chakoteya (Chrissie's Transcripts) | chakoteya.net
    the go-to fan transcripts (TOS/TAS/TNG/DS9/VOY/ENT & films).

    ST-Minutiae | st-minutiae.com
    Impressive collection of details, trivia, and articles.

    Forgotten Trek | forgottentrek.com
    deep behind-the-scenes histories, concept art, & making-of features.

    The Trek Collective | thetrekcollective.com
    long-running blog with book/comic/merch news plus schedules & guides.

    TrekNews.net | treknews.net
    frequent news, previews & reviews (including books & comics).

    More coming soon...
